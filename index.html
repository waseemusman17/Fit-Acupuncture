Feeling “Fit” is both a physical and a mental state. When in pain we are affected at a physical level by discomfort & impaired movement, and at a mental level by increased stress, poor sleep, and emotional distress.

The powerful tools of Traditional Chinese Medicine include a comprehensive system that treats both the Body & Mind. Acupuncture and Tui-Na massage promote circulation to eliminate pain and balance the nervous system; reduce the perception of stress, improve sleep, and balance our emotions so that we can heal.

BIOGRAPHY

Tyler Martin, L.Ac.

has been practicing Chinese Medicine for over 15 years and is a board certified acupuncturist and graduate of the American College of Traditional Chinese Medicine in San Francisco, CA.

He holds a Masters of Science degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine and a “Diplomate of Oriental Medicine” by the National Certification Commission of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (NCCAOM).

About

Welcome to Fit Acupuncture!

Fit Acupuncture is the private practice of Tyler Martin, Licensed Acupuncturist and Eastern Medicine Practitioner with over 15 years of experience in the field.

Tyler utilizes a specialized combination of Acupuncture and Tui-Na (Traditional Chinese massage) to promote circulation and balance the nervous system in a safe and effective method to eliminate pain.
